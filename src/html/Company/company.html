About the company

Ayni Rapé

Ayni Rape is a company specializing in the sale of the traditional South American product rapé, known for its healing and spiritual properties. We offer our customers high-quality rapé blends created with respect for the ancient traditions of the shamanic tribes of the Amazon.

Ayni Rape is built on respect for ancient traditions and the desire to bring this unique product to the modern world. We collaborate with tribes who pass on their knowledge and recipes to us. Each rapé blend is handcrafted with care for nature and compliance with environmental standards.
